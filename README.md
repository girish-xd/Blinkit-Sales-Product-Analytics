# Blinkit Sales & Product Analytics

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project on Blinkit's grocery sales dataset using Python. The project analyzes sales trends, outlet performance, customer preferences, and product-level insights to derive data-driven business recommendations.

---

## Project Overview

This project focuses on understanding how product and sales data can be leveraged to generate meaningful business insights for a quick-commerce platform. The analysis covers product categories, outlet performance, customer ratings, and sales distribution across multiple dimensions.

---

## Tech Stack

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- OpenPyXL
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Key Analyses Performed

- Sales by Product Category (Item Type)
- Sales by Item Fat Content
- Sales Trend by Outlet Establishment Year
- Sales by Outlet Size
- Sales by Outlet Location Type
- Customer Rating Analysis
- Data Cleaning and Standardization

---

## Key Product & Business Insights

- Product categories contribute differently to overall sales performance.
- Outlet size and location significantly impact revenue generation.
- Customer preferences vary across product types and fat content categories.
- Sales trends differ across outlet establishment years.
- Customer ratings provide valuable signals for understanding product quality and demand patterns.

---

## Data-Driven Business Recommendations

- Prioritize inventory planning for high-performing product categories.
- Optimize product assortment based on outlet demographics.
- Leverage customer preference insights for product positioning strategies.
- Identify underperforming outlet segments and improve sales initiatives.
- Use sales and rating trends to support business decisions.
